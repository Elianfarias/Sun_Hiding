# ☀️ Sun Hiding

Sun Hiding is a stylish 2D side-scrolling action platformer. Slash through waves of monsters, absorb their souls, and unlock the next level by collecting them all. Every encounter is a test of reflexes — dodge attacks with your dash, and strike back with precise attacks.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

Explore dark, corrupted levels filled with enemies. Kill them to absorb their souls — collect enough to unlock the portal to the next stage. Watch your health bar and keep your infection level under control by avoiding toxic fog zones or resting at bonfires. You only have 4 bullets per reload, so make every shot count.

---

## ⚔️ Features

- Responsive combat with fast dashes and clean hit feedback
- Directional charged jump with parabolic trajectory preview
- 4-bullet magazine system with manual reload
- Infection system — toxic fog raises your contamination bar. Reach 100% and you die
- Soul collection mechanic to advance between levels
- Slow motion mode at the cost of vitality
- Atmospheric pixel-art style with dynamic visual effects

---

## 🕹️ Controls

| Action | Control |
|---|---|
| Move | A / D |
| Jump / Charged Jump | Space (tap / hold toward mouse) |
| Attack | Left Mouse Button |
| Dash | Left Shift |
| Reload | R |
| Slow Motion | E |
